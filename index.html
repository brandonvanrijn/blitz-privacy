Blitz Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-30

Blitz is an offline flashcard app. The summary is simple: Blitz does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you create — decks, flashcards, review history, your name, your profile picture, your bio, your streak — stays on your device.

This policy explains the full picture, in plain language.

1. What data Blitz stores

Blitz stores the following entirely on your device, in the app's local storage:

  • Decks and the flashcards inside them (including any images you attach)
  • Your review history and study streak
  • A name you optionally set
  • A profile picture you optionally pick from your photo library
  • A bio you optionally write
  • Your settings (theme, language, daily reminder time, notifications on/off)

This data never leaves your device. There is no Blitz account. There is no Blitz server.

2. What data Blitz does not collect

  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reports
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, calendars, microphone, or camera (Blitz requests access to the photo library only when you tap "Choose photo" for a profile picture or a custom deck icon)
  • No third-party SDKs that phone home

3. Photo library access

When you tap "Choose photo" for a profile picture or a custom deck icon, iOS asks for permission to access your photo library. The chosen image is copied into Blitz's local storage. It is never uploaded anywhere. You can revoke photo access at any time in iOS Settings → Blitz → Photos.

4. Notifications

You can opt in to a daily review reminder. This is a local notification scheduled by iOS on your device. No notification server is involved. You can turn this off at any time in Blitz Settings or iOS Settings → Blitz → Notifications.

5. In-app purchase

Blitz offers a one-time €5 unlock for Premium. The purchase is processed entirely by the App Store. Apple sends Blitz a receipt confirming the purchase status; Blitz uses this to unlock Premium features locally. Apple's billing privacy policy applies to the payment itself.

6. Children

Blitz does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because Blitz collects no data at all, this is structural — not a policy promise.

7. Your rights (GDPR, CCPA, and similar)

Because Blitz holds none of your data, traditional data-subject requests (access, deletion, portability) are trivial:

  • Access: Open Blitz. Your data is what you see.
  • Portability: Use Settings → Export to download all your data as a file.
  • Deletion: Settings → Reset all data, or delete the app from your device.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in any meaningful way, the "Last updated" date at the top will change and the new version will be linked from inside the app.
